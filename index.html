About HAS

A homeroom you never had to outgrow.

Most of us stopped learning about ourselves the day we started taking care of everyone else. HAS reopens that classroom — not for a diploma, but for the parts of you that got left on a shelf.

Mission

Give African women 30+ a structured, guided space to heal, learn, and grow.

Vision

A generation of women who model reinvention for the ones watching them.

Philosophy

Growth belongs in community, not isolation — so nobody does this alone.
